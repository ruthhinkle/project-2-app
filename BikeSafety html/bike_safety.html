<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Print copies of the Chicago Bike Map are usually available at local bike shops all over the city. The print 2020 map is currently available in only a handful of shops. However, you can check it out online at the link above.<br />
</span></h4>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: 14pt;">If you are a U.S. resident living <em>outside Chicago</em>, you can request a bike map by <a href="https://chicagocompletestreets.org/sample-page-2/contactus/bike-maps/">mail</a>.</span></h4>
